About Me

Hey there, amazing people! I’m Divya, a 23-year-old coder, coffee-lover, and tech enthusiast currently figuring out life through a Master’s in Computer Science. 🎓💻 Let’s be real—I’m head over heels for web development. 🌐 It’s where creativity and logic collide for me, and I get to craft websites that not only work but feel warm and welcoming. I’ll be honest: I’m still learning (and bookmarking tutorials like my life depends on it). But I’m fueled by passion and curiosity. 🚀 Sometimes it feels overwhelming, but hey, doesn’t everything worth doing feels the same for the majority of us? I’m big on structured goals and to-do lists because nothing beats that “cross it off the list” satisfaction. ✅ Still, I’m all about surprises—my life motto is, “Plan hard, but let life play its wild cards.” 🌊 So yeah, I’m quirky, curious, and maybe a little too in love with to-do lists—but that’s me! Let’s connect and create something awesome together. 🌟

My Blogs

📚 My First Coding Challenge

Ah, the thrill of completing my very first coding challenge! It was 100 Days of Code in Python. While the initial days felt easy, things got progressively intense. In 42 days (yes, I finished early!), I emerged more confident and ready to tackle more Python adventures. 🐍

🌐 Journey into HTML & CSS

Back in the day of online classes, I dived headfirst into the world of web development. Armed with an Udemy course, I built tiny projects based on every single topic I learned. Seven days later, I was making my own designs and loving the process. It was my gateway into the web dev universe. 🚀

🎨 30 Days of HTML & CSS Challenge

Fast forward, I took the 30 Days HTML & CSS Challenge by Codedamn. It pushed me to explore advanced topics, and yes, Google was my best friend throughout. From researching cool features to implementing them, this challenge honed my skills and made me laugh at all those developer-Google memes. 😂
